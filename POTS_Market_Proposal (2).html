Part 01 · Phần 01

Project Overview
Tổng Quan Dự Án

🇬🇧 English

POTS Market is an ambitious decentralized infrastructure project combining two powerhouses of Web3 finance: DeFi yield generation (via POTS.money) and a decentralized prediction market (via POTS.market). Built on BNB Chain and integrated with PancakeSwap, the protocol positions itself as the world's largest decentralized prediction public infrastructure — a platform where users can earn, predict, and grow alongside a global community.

🇻🇳 Tiếng Việt

POTS Market là dự án hạ tầng phi tập trung kết hợp hai trụ cột của Web3: sinh lợi DeFi (thông qua POTS.money) và thị trường dự đoán phi tập trung (thông qua POTS.market). Xây dựng trên BNB Chain, tích hợp PancakeSwap, giao thức định vị là hạ tầng dự đoán công cộng phi tập trung lớn nhất thế giới — nơi người dùng kiếm lãi, tham gia dự đoán và cùng phát triển với cộng đồng toàn cầu.

$IBS

Derivative Bond Contract Token

  • Elastic supply — algorithmic inflation/deflation
  • Issuance: Algorithmic Derivative Bond Mechanism
  • Used for: bond issuance, staking, network rewards
  • LP pair: IBS / USDT (50/50 via POL)
  • Sells incur 2.5% fee on PancakeSwap
$POTS

Prediction Market Native Token

  • Fixed supply: 21,000,000 POTS (Bitcoin-inspired)
  • Issuance: Liquidity Pool Auction
  • Used for: governance, incentives, prediction market
  • LP pair: POTS / BTC
  • Daily 0.5% deflation mechanism from POTS/BTC pool
How Money Flows · Dòng Tiền Vận Hành
💧
DeFi LP Pool
POTS.money

Users deposit USDT → smart contract forms 50/50 LP (USDT+IBS) → locked via POL protocol → generates compounding yield paid in IBS every 8 hours.

🏦
Co-Building Treasury
CBT Pool

USDT contributions fund POTS token auctions. BTC is added daily via PBM activation. POTS burns when swapped for BTC — creating price upward linkage.

🔮
Prediction Market Fees
POTS.market

2% of all prediction market topic fees flow back into the IBS LP Pool — creating a self-reinforcing demand cycle between the two ecosystems.

🌐
Network Incentives
IBS Rewards

VIP network members receive a percentage of their sub-network's total staking/bonding activity as daily IBS rewards — capped at 10% of own holdings.

Part 04 · Phần 04

Community Incentive
Chính Sách Cộng Đồng & Hoa Hồng

🇬🇧 Mechanism

POTS operates a dual-reward community system. The IBS Liquidity Expansion Incentive rewards leaders based on the total active accounts in their sub-network — paid daily in IBS, capped at 10% of their own holdings. The POTS Consensus Formation Incentive distributes a portion of the daily 0.5% POTS/BTC pool deflation equally across all VIP tiers and global active holders.

🇻🇳 Cơ Chế

POTS vận hành hệ thống cộng đồng hai tầng. IBS Liquidity Expansion Incentive thưởng leaders dựa trên tổng tài khoản active trong mạng lưới — trả hàng ngày bằng IBS, tối đa 10% holdings bản thân. POTS Consensus Formation Incentive phân phối một phần giảm phát 0.5%/ngày từ pool POTS/BTC đều cho tất cả các cấp VIP và holders toàn cầu.
